About Me

I'm Lewis, I am a freelance Front-End Web Designer/Developer. I'm based just outside of Glasgow, Scotland, UK. I have used the name Odysseyweb as my brand name as I thought it was better than using my actual name, how many lewises do you know?

I started coding at the age of 16 and I'm now 24, I'm getting old! I have always been interested in Front-end Development since I first learned about it and it led me to attend Glasgow Clyde College for Web Engineering which I thoroughly enjoyed and I learned a lot. From how to use frameworks, version management, hosting, development, design, UI, UX, team management, the list feels endless.

Now to get to the year 2022 I have decided I finally wish to pursue my dream of becoming a Freelance Front-End Developer. Although I am not taking clients just now, I hope you keep me in mind for the near future as on my side there’s still a couple of things I need to iron out before I go live – but the fact you’re here reading this anyway is great!
