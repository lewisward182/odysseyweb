Shopify

Here's just some of the things that Shopify offer and what I can do to help your new or existing business.

  • Shopify offers a great way to attain a responsive fully customizable site with its theme editor and admin pannel
  • You can list your products for sale along with size, colours, description and images
  • Connecting to other services such as printful
  • Run marketing campaigns, you can build up a mailing list, this is extremely useful to generate interest and sales, maybe send your customers a one off discount or information on a new or upcoming product
  • You can view a list of all your customers in one place
  • Without customer retention your store will generate no sales, Shopify helps you keep ontop of this. You can view abandoned checkouts, ordered items, returned items etc
  • View your sites Analytics, this is extremely useful to have a good understanding on the customer journey throughout their time on your site
  • Search Enginge Optimisation is extremely important, it's how you are discovered. Shopify gives you an easy way to manage this
  • Social Media Intigration is arguably one of the most important parts to a website, letting your customer know where to find you on alternate platforms it also gives you a sense of legitimacy if you're just starting out
  • You can control and manage things like tax and shipping directly from the admin pannel
  • You can also add staff memebers, this is useful if you're a small team. You can control what they can and can't do.
  • Shopify gives you a great and easy way to set up policies for shipping, privacy and returns with their own templates or you can create your own.
